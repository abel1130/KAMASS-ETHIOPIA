Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and support options.

We offer same-day delivery within Addis Ababa city center and next-day delivery to surrounding areas including Akaki Kality. For remote areas, delivery may take 2-3 business days. All orders over 5,000 ETB qualify for free shipping.

Yes, our technical support team in Addis Ababa is fluent in both English and Amharic. You can request support in your preferred language when contacting our team via phone, email, or in-person at our Akaki Kality office.

We accept multiple payment methods including Telebirr, CBE Birr, Amole, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. For business purchases, we also offer invoice payment terms with proper documentation.

All products come with a standard manufacturer warranty. Most electronics have a 1-year warranty, while premium products may have extended coverage. Warranty service is provided at our Addis Ababa service center with proof of purchase.

Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, government agencies, and businesses in Ethiopia. Please contact our sales team at the Akaki Kality office with your official documentation to discuss bulk purchase options and special pricing.
